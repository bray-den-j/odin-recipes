Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Perfect fudgy brownies

This homemade fudgy brownie recipe is the best I've made. Since we use cocoa powder, these brownies are easy to make and so much better than the boxed mixes!

Ingredients:

  • 145g unsalted butter
  • 250g granulated sugar
  • 75g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 large cold eggs
  • 65g all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • 80g cups chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Line the bottom and sides of 8-inch baking pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Add the butter to a medium saucepan. Place over medium-low heat and cook until the butter melts completely, then turn off the heat.
  3. While the butter is hot, stir in the sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, and salt. Stir well until blended.
  4. Add the cold eggs, one at a time, stirring vigorously after each egg.
  5. When the batter looks thick and well blended, add the flour.
  6. Stir in nuts or chocolate chips (optional)
  7. Spread the thick brownie batter evenly in the prepared pan.
  8. Bake the brownies until the edges look dry and the middle is slightly underbaked, 20 to 30 minutes.
  9. Let sit for about 30 minutes before cutting.
  10. Enjoy!
