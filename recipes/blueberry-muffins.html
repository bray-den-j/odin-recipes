Easy Blueberry Muffins!

So what's up about these muffins?

My family has been making these incredible blueberry muffins for years! Our recipe is so quick and easy that you can have warm homemade blueberry muffins in less than 30 minutes. These muffins are perfect to whip up and bake the night before a long work week. Just grab and go!

Ingredients:

  • 195g all-purpose flour
  • 150g granulated sugar, plus 1 tablespoon for muffin tops
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 80ml vegetable or avocado oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 80ml milk or milk substitute
  • 1 ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cub fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Add oil to separate bowl, then crack the egg and pour in the milk. Add the vanilla extract and whisk until combined.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir gently until the batter comes together.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the muffin cups. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until tops are no longer wet and a toothpick inserted into the muffin comes out with crumbs, not batter.
  6. Enjoy!
